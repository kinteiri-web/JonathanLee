# Glossary

## Personal Blog

The whole Hugo site. Its job is to publish research notes, paper-reading articles, and personal records with a stable archive.

## Post

A dated article under `content/post`. Posts are shown on the homepage and archives.

## Paper Reading

A structured reading note for one paper. It should include paper metadata, the main claim, figure-by-figure interpretation when useful, limitations, and the author's own questions.

## Research

The broad category for academic and technical writing. It includes paper reading, experiment notes, technical design notes, and research ideas.

## Draft

Content with `draft: true`. Drafts remain in the repository but do not appear in the generated public site unless Hugo is run with draft rendering enabled.

## Link Page

A curated page for external profiles and resources. It should contain real personal links, not theme documentation.

## ADR

Architecture Decision Record. A short document that captures one durable decision, why it was made, and what tradeoffs it creates.
