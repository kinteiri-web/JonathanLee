# ADR 0004: Content Index Pages Without Right Sidebar

Date: 2026-06-21

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The site navigation should start from broad categories and gradually reveal more specific content. On index-style pages, the right sidebar widgets duplicate this model with search, archives, category lists, and tag clouds. They make the first browsing experience less minimal.

Long article pages still benefit from the right table of contents, especially for paper-reading posts.

## Decision

Remove the right sidebar from:

- Homepage
- Recent updates page
- All categories page
- Individual category list pages

Keep the article page right sidebar/table of contents unchanged.

## Consequences

The entry and browsing pages are visually simpler and focus on the category-first navigation model.

Long articles keep their reading aid, so paper-reading notes remain easy to navigate.
